Balsm is an open, community-driven platform for healthcare innovation. Our mission is to empower clinics, practitioners, and developers with modern, ethical, and accessible digital health tools.

- 🌍 Open Source & Commercial Licensing
- 🏥 Modular, privacy-first healthcare platform
- 🤝 Collaboration with clinicians, researchers, and technologists
- 🚀 Building for real-world impact in emerging markets and beyond

Explore our repositories for EHR, practice management, patient engagement, and more. Join us in shaping the future of digital health!
